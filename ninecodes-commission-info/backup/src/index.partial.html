COMPATIBILITY

All skins are designed and coded for Google Chrome. Some aspects may not show up on other browsers (scrollbars on Firefox, etc.) so please be mindful of that.

I work in a 1920x1080 resolution environment, so therefore my skins will look best in that resolution. I will however, accommodate smaller-width screens always, and will try my best to adapt to your needs.

Currently I do not accept commissions for dynamic skinning.

PAYMENT

Prices vary depending on the complexity of the project. I have three different packages available, each with varying levels of details and are priced accordingly, so you can select whichever package best fits your needs and/or budget.

All fees should be paid upfront when the commission is completed and ready to be handed over, so please prepare accordingly. The skin will be transferred once payment is submitted and approved.

The commissioned skin, once completed and finished, is non-refundable; however, you may cancel your commission at any time before full payment has been made. Please note that a commission that has been cancelled can and will be repurposed for a premade sale.
