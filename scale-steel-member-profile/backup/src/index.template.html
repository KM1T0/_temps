lineage.

the character's ancestry, their pedigree, and any other circumstances of their birth.

Born as the only child of nobleman Sir Francis Justize Valmont and wife Lady Evelyn Trellis Valmont, her family has close ties to the Magisterium and as a result, was born with a silver spoon and a privileged life. Unfortunately, she was also born blind, and this setback has caused quite a heartache for her family.

They have tried everything to cure her lack of sight; from bathing in holy waters, to fervent praying, to even alchemical concoctions of questionable origins, the Valmonts paid every gold coin they could to try restore her sight. But it was clear that this was Aelfric's Will.

It was customary for most Valmont children to marry into the Farnessan line, and she was to be betrothed to one of the Archbishop's children. However, she ran away from home on the eve of her wedding, never to be seen again... that was three years ago.

training.

the formal education, instruction, and/or any additional disciplines they have learned in the past.

Highly-versed in the religious scriptures, trained in proper Farnessan decorum, manners, and the socialite's way of life. Anything that could be ingrained into her despite her lack of sight, was drilled into her by force. She cannot sew, cook, paint, or pursue other hobbies and interests girls her age would do that requires the eyes, but she can dance, sing, and play certain instruments by touch and feel alone.

Since then, she has been trained as an assassin by the Obsidian Wings after they've taken her in after she had left home. Despite only having been with the Wings for a few, short years, she has shown a quickness to adapt and her eagerness to learn allowed her to flourish as an expert assassin.

reputation.

the general public or community's opinion and estimation from the character's past acts and deeds.

Before she left home, she was virtually unknown in the Farnessan court as her parents tried to hide the shame that was their disabled daughter from the prying eyes of the public. When her marriage was announced, she was the talk of the town; of both good and bad rumours.

Her reputation only took a turn for the worst after deserting her husband on the night of her wedding, never to be seen again. With her family disgraced, they have struck her names from the records and as of now, the Valmont family 'officially' has never had a daughter.

She has basic control over dark magic, but they are used primarily as a crutch to her blindness and some more. When she is shrouded in darkness, she can actually 'see' better due to the shadows acting as a sonar map to her senses.

Extremely acrobatic, lithe, and athletic despite being raised as a frail, young noblewoman for most of her life. She shows extreme potential for adaptation, learning new things, and fighting techniques. She is not a master of any particular school of martial arts or training, but it seems like her actions are an amalgamation of common sense, instinct, and talent.

She is also quite proficient with Atman-styled blades, throwing daggers, and metal wires. She has some knowledge of poisons, but her sight prevents her from mastering it completely.
